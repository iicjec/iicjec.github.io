Youth Discussion


Description

A panel discussion on topics related to innovation and creativity among youth.


Theme

Any Innovative topic, such as Innovative ideas to boost up Economy/ Employment in India, Recent innovations around the world, etc.


Event Guidelines

1. A short discussion event in which participants will be provided a topic to discuss.
2. Each participant will have 1-2 minutes to speak on the given topic.
3. E-Certificates will be provided to the participants.

Judgement Criteria:
Winners will be selected on the basis of :

1.Communication Skills
2. How Innovative is your suggestion, etc.
*Every participant will get a E-certificate.
