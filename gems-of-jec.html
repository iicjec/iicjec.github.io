GEMS of JEC


Description

It is a one to one interaction between our passed out seniors and Host .
This event is divided into 6 fields :
GATE , CAT , GRE , UPSC , PLACEMENT , STARTUPS .
This event will be of 3 days 2 sessions each day .
The host will questionnaire them about their field .


Theme

Innovation (innovative thinking in tackling real life problems )
Host will ask their sweet as well bitter experiences , strategies in finding way in opposite situations , outlook towards future , suggestions for other students , current scenario of their fields , how to deal with corona - effects on studies , etc.


Result Declaration Criteria:
Winners will be selected on the basis of :

1.Entries will be judged purely on the basis of originality of the piece, creativity and innovation put together in their work.
2. Entries will be accepted in the form of images, PDF and document.
3.Results will be declared after the last event.

*Every participant will get a E-certificate.
*Winner's will get a certificate of merit.


Timings :

27-29nov (4-5 and 630-730pm for all 3 days )
