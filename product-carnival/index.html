Session 2

(Pitching Session)


This event will provide a platform for the participants to present their product ideas in the form of a presentation. It aims to provide future entrepreneurs with an experience to sell their dummy product/services successfully. It would help them to understand the operation of start-ups in corporate world on a small scale by presenting their products


Guidelines


1.) A live presentation round will be held, where participants will present their solutions in an interactive way in front of the judges panel.
2.) Presentation time will be 10 mins max for each participant/team, accompanied by a 5 mins questionnaire round.
3.) PPT can be with or without audio containing not more than 10 slides.
4.) However following slides are compulsory :
a. Team Details
b. Introduction of product idea
c. Product plan and prototype
d. Uniqueness
5.) Cost effective and easily executable ideas will score better.
6.) Single participants or teams of maximum 3 members can register.
7.) Participants can learn from examples but plagiarism is prohibited.

*Every participant will get an E-certificate of participation after filling a feedback form.


Timing :

23rd June 2021
