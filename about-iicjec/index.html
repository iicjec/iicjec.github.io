ABOUT IIC-JEC

MHRDs Innovation Cell has envisioned creation of ❛Institution’s Innovation Council (IICs)❜ across selected Higher Education Institutions.

An Institute Innovation Council (IIC) has been established at JEC Jabalpur ,as per the norms and directives of Ministry of Human Resource Development (MHRD) Innovation Cell (MIC).

IIC aims to enhance the culture of innovation and incubation among faculty and students at JEC, leading to successful JEC startups, promoted and owned by the faculties and students.

Currently, the council consists of 15 student members, 5 faculty members and 2 external members, with Prof.Shailja Shukla as the President.

IIC will be organising various events in the Institute campus; for example- Webinars, contests, Leadership talks, Workshops and Hackathons for faculty, students and staff .

Ultimately, the aim is to conduct various innovation, entrepreneurship related activities and to systematically foster the culture of innovation in JEC Jabalpur.
