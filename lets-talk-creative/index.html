Lets Talk Creative


Description

This live show aims at providing an eminent platform to young achievers to express and share about their accomplishments, skills acquired, during the lockdown period. It will last for 2 days . We believe such events encourage entrepreneurial mindset amongst students.


Guidelines

1. An online Session will be conducted through Google Meet platform.
2. Any kind of misconduct will not be tolerated.
*Every participant will get an E-certificate.
