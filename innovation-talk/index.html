Innovation Talk


Description

This event follows the panel discussion pattern, much like a debate, but much more constructive in nature. It aims to encourage productive creative discussion among participants. 4-5 topics are decided and a time fixed for each. Participants are picked from the audiences and each one is given a span to present ideas. Putting down other participants' ideas is discouraged.


Topics

1. Future of Crypto? Doge a universal currency?
2. War on privacy? Is it even important? Shorts, Reels leading to ADHD? Bad for us?
3. Twitter, a news portal? Reliable? Legible?
4. Work from home? Will it last?


Guidelines
1. A discussion among participants from various interests on innovation and creativity.
2. 2 hour discussion where 4 topics will be discussed seperately in different slots among all the participants.
3. Present scenario won't be discussed in detail, participant is supposed to give their opinion regarding future aspescts.
4. No Languages constraints.
5. Audience and participants can attend all slots if they want. Timing will be specified.

*Every participant will get an E-certificate.
